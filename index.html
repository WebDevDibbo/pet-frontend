FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How does the pet adoption process work?

The adoption process involves browsing available pets, filling out an adoption form, and meeting the pet. Once your application is approved, you can bring your new pet home!

2. Are all pets vaccinated and spayed/neutered?

Yes, all pets are vaccinated and spayed/neutered before they are made available for adoption to ensure their health and well-being.

3. Can I adopt if I live in a rented home?

Yes, but you may need to provide proof that your landlord allows pets. We aim to ensure every pet is placed in a suitable, pet-friendly environment.

4. Are there any fees for adopting a pet?

Some pets are free, but others may have an adoption fee to cover vaccinations, medical checkups, and other care services provided before adoption.

5. Can I return the pet if it doesn't work out?

Yes, we understand that sometimes things don't work out. We have a return policy to ensure that the pet can be rehomed safely.
